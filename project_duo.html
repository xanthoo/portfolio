    Project development

    What I have done in order to satisfy the learning outcomes


    Vision:

    During this DUO project, I have worked a lot on my vision, especially when it comes to impacting society and relationship with technology. During the brain storming pats of this project, I found myself steering into trying to help impaired people. For example, I wanted to make a hand gesturing keyboard by using the accelerator sensor and the raspberry pi, and the other idea was an eye-tracking keyboard meant for people who cannot use their arms, so the eye tracker would track the person's eyes and after standing on top of a certain letter, it will type it out. I have also worked on making sketches, discussing design ideas and making the product MLP by adding screen savers, ambient sounds and painting the frame, as well as adding "sad-face" specific compliments that would hopefully cheer the person up.

    Interactive user experience:

    I have researched extensively what the user experience should be, and came to the conclusion that the user should do as little as possible for the product to deliver what its expected to. We have also made a scaled-down installation, which resembles the ideal product in our vision.
    ====================================================================================


    Prototypes:

    I have prototyped in both software and hardware. I have made sure that the code works as intended, by doing usability tests and following standard testing procedure. I have also created user stories, use cases and an activity diagram. On top of partially creating the MVP, I am also in charge of creating the MLP of the product, which requires many changes, trying and failing. I'm also in charge of makin the final prototype which should resamble closest the real ideal product.
